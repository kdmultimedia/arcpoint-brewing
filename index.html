Our Story

Built on
Brotherhood &
Craft


Arcpoint was born out of friendship, military discipline, and a shared dream. CJ Eldridge and Chris Peterson — veterans of Westover Air Reserve Base — started brewing together years before Belchertown even had a bylaw that allowed breweries to exist.

They pushed through a global pandemic, fought for permits, and poured years of free time into building something real. With Dave Paré joining as co-owner in 2022, the three finally opened Arcpoint's taproom — Belchertown's first brewery — to lines out the door.

The taproom occupies a two-story space with a mezzanine overlooking the brewing equipment, a patio with picnic tables, and a vibe that's entirely its own. Dogs welcome. Community required.
